About

// Thomas YB Wright

Thomas YB Wright is a Gallatin, Tennessee-based artist working across music, photography, and reflective systems of awareness.

"My music speaks for itself through live flow. My struggles, pain and anger is different from yours. I was born in Tennessee. I'm a product of my surroundings. Good vibes lover, positive thinkers crew."

He creates from lived experience — turning observation into structured creative systems. His work is not about prediction. It is about awareness.

  • Emotional expression through music
  • Visual storytelling through photography
  • Pattern recognition through Seeds of Astrology
  • W.E Wright Entertainment — uniting artists
